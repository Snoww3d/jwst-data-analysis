Cards

Three kinds: TargetCard (discovery), RecipeCard (suggested composites), DataCard (library). All share the same surface, radius, and border tokens — the differences are in pin badges, thumbnails, and meta rows.

Target cards

4:3 thumbnail, title + catalog id, meta row, potential badge. Hover lifts 2 px. Thumbnail falls back to a category gradient + telescope icon when imagery is missing.
