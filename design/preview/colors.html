Colors

JWST Discovery is dark-first. Four surface steps from page to hover, accents used sparingly and intentionally, and a dedicated instrument palette that encodes scientific meaning — MIRI red, NIRCam blue, NIRISS green, NIRSpec amber.

Filter swatches in use

When filter chips appear on a recipe card, each has a colored dot. Colors are mapped per-recipe (approximating the filter's wavelength) and passed in at render time — not a global palette.
