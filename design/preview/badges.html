Badges & Chips

Status pills, pinned corner badges, instrument tags, filter chips, and selectable tags — all the small semantic containers the product uses.

Status badges

One-word status. Pill shape, 24 px high, translucent fill + full-color text. Used for "Ready / Login required / Mosaic needed / Indexed".

Success Ready Indexed Great for composites
Warning Login required Mosaic needed Limited data
Error Failed No observations
Info Good for composites L3
Neutral Uncategorized Draft

Pinned corner badges

Solid fill, sit in the top-right corner of a card, flush to the top edge with rounded bottom corners only. Reserved for "Recommended" + curated gradient badges.

Instrument badges

Monospace, bordered, color-coded. The encoding is scientifically meaningful — keep instruments on their assigned colors.

NIRCam · blue NIRCAM Near-Infrared Camera · 0.6–5 µm
MIRI · red MIRI Mid-Infrared Instrument · 5–28 µm
NIRISS · green NIRISS Near-Infrared Imager & Slitless Spec
NIRSpec · amber NIRSPEC Near-Infrared Spectrograph · 0.6–5 µm

Calibration level

Shown in observation tables. L3 is the emphasized state (fully calibrated, ready for science).

L1 L2a L2b L3

Filter chips

Always uppercase, always monospace, always with a colored swatch dot at the wavelength color.

Selectable chips

Used for category / instrument / filter selections. Hover = border+text bump; Active = full accent border + tint fill.
