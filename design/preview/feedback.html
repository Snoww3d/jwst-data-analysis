Feedback & overlays

Modal, toast, progress, empty state, tooltip. These are the five transient surfaces every JWST Discovery flow reaches for. Shared vocabulary: dark surface, 8-px radius on containers, single 3-px left border for semantic edge, ease-out motion.

Modal

One decision at a time. Header / body / footer. Footer is right-aligned, primary action last. Destructive variant marks the header with a red rail. Page chrome blurs behind; no modals over modals.

Toast

Transient, stackable, upper-right on the viewport. Four tones: info · success · warning · error. One short sentence, optional action link, optional dismiss. Auto-dismiss at 5 s for info/success; sticky for warning/error until acknowledged.

Empty state

Never blank. Every empty container gets a muted icon, a sentence of what this space is, one sentence of what to do about it, and a CTA. Dashed border distinguishes empty containers from loaded ones.
